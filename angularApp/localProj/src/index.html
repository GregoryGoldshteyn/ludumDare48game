Bat and Spider Cavern

Help Charlie find his spellbook in the dark

Controls:

Mouse: Use to aim and left or right click to shoot

Keyboard: W A S D to move up, left, down, or right respectively

Press ESCAPE at any time to regain control of the mouse

Instructions:

Get to the bottom of the cavern without dying

Try to rack up a high score

5 hits, and it's game over!

Lore:

Charlie the Wizard dropped his spell book down Spider and Bat Caverns. Help him retrieve it using the only spell he knows off hand: magic missile. Join him and his hat on this deep dive into adventure!
